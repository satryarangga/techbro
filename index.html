About Me

Hey there! I'm Rangga, the one-man tech powerhouse behind Techbro Studio.

Your One-Stop Technology Partner

Over the past 15 years, I've worked with both fast-growing startups and large companies, helping them build successful digital products. I specialize in creating websites and web applications that are not only beautiful but also easy to use and maintain. My experience allows me to quickly understand your business needs and translate them into effective digital solutions.

I've helped numerous businesses launch their mobile apps, making sure they work smoothly on both iPhone and Android devices. My approach focuses on creating apps that are fast, reliable, and provide a great user experience. Whether you're looking to build something new or improve your existing app, I can help you achieve your goals efficiently.

I understand that technology can be complex, which is why I focus on making it simple and accessible for your business. I help companies move their operations to the cloud, making their systems more secure and easier to manage. My goal is to build solutions that grow with your business, ensuring you get the most value from your technology investments.
